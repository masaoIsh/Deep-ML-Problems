Q: In epsilon greedy action selection, for the case of two actions and epsilon=0.5, what is the probability that the greedy action is selected?
A: Let G denote greedy action selection and E denote exploration selection. Then, from the law of total probability, P(G)=P(G and E)+P(G and E^c)=P(G|E)P(E)+P(G|E^c)P(E^c)=0.5(0.5)+1(0.5)=0.25+0.5=0.75
